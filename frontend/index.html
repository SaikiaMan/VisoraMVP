Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Visora

Is Visora free to use?

expand_more
Yes! Visora offers a free tier that includes core features like AI doubt solving, note generation, and quiz creation. Premium features and unlimited access are available with a paid subscription.

Which subjects and topics are supported?

expand_more
Visora supports a wide range of subjects including Science, Mathematics, History, English, Economics, and more. We continuously expand our subject library. Any YouTube lecture across subjects can be used with Visora.

How accurate is the AI?

expand_more
Our AI has been trained on quality educational content and is designed specifically for learning. Accuracy improves as you use it more—the system learns from your feedback and interaction patterns.

Can I use videos other than YouTube?

expand_more
Currently, Visora works best with YouTube videos. We're working on expanding support for other video platforms and direct uploads.

How do I track my progress?

expand_more
Your learning dashboard shows detailed analytics including quiz performance, weak topics, study time, and progress over time. All your data is saved and accessible anytime.

Is my data private and secure?

expand_more
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your data is private and never shared with third parties. You have full control over your account and data.
